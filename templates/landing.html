About SYNTH.AI

SYNTH.AI is your personalized AI-powered music DJ built specifically for Bollywood lovers. It reads your day, mood, and preferences to generate a unique playlist – every single time. Built with Gemini AI, Flask, and Spotify APIs, it's designed to vibe with your feelings.

How It Works

Mood-Based Input

You tell SYNTH.AI how your day went, what you're feeling, and what you're up to.

Gemini + Flask AI

Under the hood, AI understands your emotions and generates a custom tracklist.

Spotify Integration

SYNTH.AI connects to Spotify to deliver your mood-matched playlist instantly.
